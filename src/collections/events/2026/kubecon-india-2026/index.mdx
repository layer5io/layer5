Join Meshery at KubeCon + CloudNativeCon India 2026!

Join the Meshery project at KubeCon + CloudNativeCon India 2026 from June 18th to June 19th, 2026 and get introduced to collaborative cloud native management and Meshery open source maintainers.

Come find the Meshery team at the Project Pavilion — stop by to meet the maintainers, get a live demo of Kanvas, and learn how Meshery is simplifying cloud native infrastructure management for teams around the world.

### About Meshery

Meshery, the open source cloud native manager, enables collaborative configuration and management of your Kubernetes clusters, workloads, and CNCF projects. With Kanvas, Meshery provides an intuitive visual interface to design, deploy, and operate cloud native infrastructure with ease.
