<h4>Contributing to Meshery Server</h4> <br />
<p>
<strong>Trainer:</strong> <a href="https://layer5.io/community/members/shlok-mishra" target="_blank">Shlok Mishra</a> <br />
<strong>Technologies:</strong> Golang, Gorilla, OpenAPI Schema
</p>

<p>
This Contributor Training Series session provides a detailed walkthrough on how to contribute to the Meshery Server. Attendees were introduced to the architecture of Meshery and its key components like Meshkit, MeshSync, and <code>meshery/schemas</code>.
</p>

<p>
The session also covered important backend concepts such as custom error handling, logging mechanisms, and Mesheryâ€™s schema-driven design philosophy. Key areas to contribute, relevant repositories, and community resources were also highlighted.
</p>

You will learn to:

<ul>
  <li>Understand Meshery Server architecture and its core components</li>
  <li>Set up and contribute to Meshery Server, Meshkit, and MeshSync</li>
  <li>Implement custom error handling and logging</li>
  <li>Use and extend OpenAPI schema-driven approaches</li>
  <li>Identify key areas of contribution and navigate related resources</li>
</ul>
