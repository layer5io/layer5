  <h3>Overview</h3>
  <p><a href="https://cloud.layer5.io/academy/overview">Layer5 Academy</a> is a structured learning platform built into Layer5 Cloud. It offers three types of content to help you skill up in cloud native technologies:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Learning Paths</strong> — Comprehensive curricula that build knowledge from the ground up, covering topics from Kubernetes fundamentals to advanced Meshery usage.</li>
    <li><strong>Challenges</strong> — Hands-on, practical exercises focused on solving specific problems in a scenario-based format. Completing a challenge earns you a digital badge.</li>
    <li><strong>Certifications</strong> — Formal assessments that validate your skills and provide recognized credentials, including the <a href="https://meshery.io/blog/2025/10/2025-10-27-certified-meshery-contributor/">Certified Meshery Contributor (CMC)</a> certification.</li>
  </ul>
  <p>All learning materials — learning paths, challenges, and exams — are completely free. The only optional paid item is the issuance of an official certificate after passing a Certification exam.</p>
  <h3>Getting Started</h3>
  <ol>
    <li>Sign up or log in at <a href="https://cloud.layer5.io/">Layer5 Cloud</a>.</li>
    <li>Navigate to the <a href="https://cloud.layer5.io/academy/overview">Academy overview</a>.</li>
    <li>Browse the catalog — use the Content Type filter to choose between Learning Paths, Challenges, and Certifications.</li>
    <li>Enroll in a learning path or challenge that matches your goals.</li>
  </ol>
  <h3>Featured Learning Paths</h3>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Cloud Native Fundamentals</strong> — Covers Kubernetes core concepts, workloads, networking, and storage. Ideal for beginners.</li>
    <li><strong>Meshery Playground Hands-on</strong> — Guided labs using <a href="https://play.meshery.io/">Meshery Playground</a> to deploy and manage cloud native infrastructure visually.</li>
    <li><strong>Contributing to Meshery</strong> — Walks through the contributor journey, from setting up your environment to submitting your first pull request.</li>
  </ul>
  <h3>Challenges</h3>
  <p>Challenges are practical exercises structured around specific problem scenarios. Each completed challenge earns a digital badge you can share on your professional profile.</p>
  <p>Browse all available challenges at <a href="https://cloud.layer5.io/academy/overview">cloud.layer5.io/academy/overview</a>.</p>
  <h3>Certified Meshery Contributor (CMC)</h3>
  <p>The CMC certification validates technical proficiency in contributing to the Meshery open source project. It consists of five exams covering Meshery's major architectural domains:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Meshery Server</li>
    <li>Meshery CLI (mesheryctl)</li>
    <li>Meshery UI</li>
    <li>Meshery Models</li>
    <li>Meshery Extensibility</li>
  </ul>
  <p>Learn more and start your certification journey at <a href="https://cloud.layer5.io/academy/overview">Layer5 Academy</a>.</p>
