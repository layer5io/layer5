<a id="Learning"><h2>Learning together</h2></a>

As a community, we have put together an excellent set of learning materials and training resources to guide you through learning about cloud native infrastructure.

You can review the <Link to="/learn">complete list of learning resources</Link> or jump straight to the type of training you prefer:

- Interactive <Link to="/learn/kanvas-labs">cloud native labs</Link>
- Free <Link to="/learn/service-mesh-books">cloud native books</Link>
- Self-paced <Link to="/learn/workshops">cloud native workshops</Link>
- Recorded training and talks on the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/c/Layer5io?sub_confirmation=1/playlists">Layer5 YouTube Channel</a>
- Multi-course, multi-mesh <Link to="/learn/learning-paths">cloud native learning paths</Link>

These materials were created to be interactive, easy to use, and free for all.

<a id="Academy"><h2>Layer5 Academy</h2></a>

<a href="https://cloud.layer5.io/academy" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Layer5 Academy</a> is a learning platform built into Layer5 Cloud. Get structured learning paths, interactive challenges, and professional certifications. Content spans beginner to advanced levels for both developers and organizations.

For more details, check out the <a href="https://docs.layer5.io/cloud/academy/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Academy documentation</a> and explore our <a href="https://github.com/orgs/layer5io/repositories?q=academy" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">GitHub collections</a>.

<a id="Eventing"><h2>Sharing what you've learned at a technical event</h2></a>

At Layer5, we participate in many different technical conferences, in international events, in online workshops, at meetups, and so on. Our engagement in these events ranges across  various cloud native and open source topics and varies between speaking, hosting workshops, managing project booths, and so on. Our engagement in these events, increases awareness about our projects and the individual community members that contribute to the projects.

A member of the Layer5 community has presented at every KubeCon since its inception. Join us at the many events we host or participate in. Check out <Link to="/community/events">Layer5 events</Link> to for a current list of events.

<a id="Blogging"><h2>Sharing what you've learned on our blog</h2></a>

Write down what you've learned, what you've built, and what experiences that you have had in the community. All community members are welcome to contribute to the <Link to="/blog">Layer5 blog</Link>.
