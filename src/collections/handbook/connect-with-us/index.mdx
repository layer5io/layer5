## Slack Community

We are a community of like-minded people with thousands of members.

Join our <a href="https://slack.layer5.io/">Slack Workspace</a> introduce yourself and interact with the community. There are different channels in the workspace to engage within.

<a href="https://layer5io.slack.com/archives/CDM0ACDM5">#general</a> - For workspace-wide communication and announcements.

<a href="https://layer5io.slack.com/archives/CFGG6U10E">#meshery</a> - For discussion on topics in and around Meshery.

<a href="https://layer5io.slack.com/archives/C017NA80S66">#meshery-adapters</a> - Discuss topics related to meshery-adapters.

<a href="https://layer5io.slack.com/archives/C011VPREG2Z">#meshery-ci</a> - For discussion on topics related to Meshery Build, Release, Workflows, DevOps.

<a href="https://layer5io.slack.com/archives/C010LFFGFFA">#meshery-meshsync</a> - For discussions on issues related to MeshSync.

<a href="https://layer5io.slack.com/archives/C0130BQ4L3T">#mesheryctl</a> - For discussions on Meshery's CLI

<a href="https://layer5io.slack.com/archives/C019426UBNY">#newcomers</a> - For welcoming first-time contributors and community newcomers.

<a href="https://layer5io.slack.com/archives/CVBGPTFJN">#performance</a> - For discussions related to measuring and evaluating cloud native performance.

<a href="https://layer5io.slack.com/archives/C012UMS2MCM">#wasm</a> - For discussions on webassembly, rust, envoy filters.

<a href="https://layer5io.slack.com/archives/C015QJKUMPU">#websites</a> - For discussions related to the UI/UX of <a href="http://layer5.io/">Layer 5</a> project sites.
