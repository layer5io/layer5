    DevRel Platform for Kubernetes and Cloud Native Content

    Are you a CNCF Ambassador, Docker Captain, AWS Hero, Google Developer Expert, or cloud native enthusiast looking to showcase your expertise and grow your following? Layer5 offers a powerful platform to create, share, and teach cloud native technologies while building your personal brand and expanding your reach.

    Visual, Collaborative Teaching Environment

    • Live Kubernetes Clusters: Give your audience hands-on experience with a collaborative live Kubernetes environment through Meshery Playground.
    • Demonstrate Complex Concepts: Explain cloud native architectures visually and interactively.
    • Multi-Player Teaching & Presentations: Conduct live demos and training sessions where participants can follow along in real-time, whether you're presenting at a conference, or leading a workshop.

    ❝ As someone who often presents complex cloud native architectures, Meshery's collaborative environment has become my go-to platform for interactive demonstrations and workshops. ❞

    Nic Jackson

    Principal Developer Advocate at HashiCorp

    Content Creation and Publishing

    • Design Cloud Native Patterns: Create and publish cloud native designs featuring any CNCF project.
    • Curate Reference Architectures: As a Solutions Engineer or Developer Relations professional, publish best practices and operational patterns for Kubernetes and cloud-native infrastructure.
    • Develop Learning Paths: Create structured educational journeys that combine theory with hands-on practice.
    • Design Challenges/Tutorials: Create interactive labs using Meshery Playground with step-by-step guides and live demonstrations on diverse cloud native concepts.

    Build Your Professional Brand

    • Public User Profile: All your published designs and reference architectures are linked to your profile, with real-time stats to track their reach and impact.
    • Showcase Your Metrics and Leaderboard Position: Track your content's performance with analytics, leaderboard rankings, and engagement metrics. Assess what's resonating with your audience and refine your developer advocacy strategy accordingly.
    • Themed Organization: Create a personalized dashboard with your organization's colors and logo, and invite members.
    • Dedicated Workspace: Maintain a centralized environment for all your content and resources.

    ❝ Layer5's platform helps me create engaging content for developers while building my professional brand. The analytics guide my content strategy and the public profile showcases my expertise to the cloud native community. ❞

    Ruth Ikegah

    Open Source Program Manager and GitHub Star

    Turn Knowledge into Credentials

    • Guided Learning Journeys: Advance your cloud native skills through Layer5 Academy, offering structured paths that combine theory with hands-on labs.
    • Interactive Challenges: Strengthen your understanding in real-world scenarios with Academy challenges, powered by Meshery.
    • Earn Recognition: Achieve Layer5 Academy certifications and badges, and showcase them directly on your DevRel profile and leaderboards.
    • Community Contribution: Contribute new tutorials, challenges, and courses to Academy, growing your professional influence in the cloud-native ecosystem.

    Recognition Program

    • Achievement Badges: Earn badges for key milestones, like streaming logs from a Kubernetes Pod for the first time.
    • Leaderboard Visibility: Participate in challenges, compete on leaderboards, and stand out in the community.
    • Featured Mentions: Get recognized when your content becomes popular, with shoutouts from the project.

    ❝ The platform helped me gain recognition in Developer Relations by showcasing my popular designs to the community. ❞

    Yash Sharma

    Layer5 MeshMate & Developer Advocate at DigitalOcean
