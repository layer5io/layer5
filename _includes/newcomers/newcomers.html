Welcome Newcomers!

Whether you are a first-time contributor or an open-source veteran, you are welcome to contribute to and actively engage in the development of projects at Layer5.

Here are some instructions to get you started and if you haven't yet, join the Slack channel to collaborate with the community.

How to contribute?

The following set of steps outlines the process by which you can openly engage, learn, and participate in the broad set of open source projects at Layer5. If at any time you get stuck, please seek help in the #first-timers channel in the Layer5 (joining instructions below). Our MeshMates and community members are here to help!
